Privacy policy

Border Force complies with the Data Protection Act 1998 Opens in new window and is responsible for when and how your personal information is collected and used.

The information you provide is used to:

  • make a decision on your application
  • help prevent and detect crime
  • help improve the service and train Border Force staff

Sharing your information

The information you provide may be shared with other people to:

  • process your application
  • check it is correct
  • help prevent and detect crime, e.g. fraud and money laundering

They may also use your information for their own purposes.

The other people that your information may be shared with include, for example:

  • fraud prevention agencies
  • UK, US or other overseas government departments or law enforcement agencies
  • people who can verify the information in your application

How information held by fraud prevention agencies may be used.

Protecting your information

Border Force will:

  • keep your information safe and secure
  • only share your information as detailed above
  • only ask for what is needed
  • only keep your information for as long as it is needed

Your information will not be made available for commercial use without your permission.
